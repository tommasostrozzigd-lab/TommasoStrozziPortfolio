Roles: 🖥️Programmer,💡Game Designer,
📊Level Designer,📜Narrative Designer

Overview:

"As the thought that can change a person's life, you have the responsibility to challenge many dangers and puzzles in order to let your owner... order his pizza."


A top-down 2D puzzle game where movement is limited to four directions, and the player slides continuously until hitting a wall. Plan carefully to find the correct path and solve each puzzle.

Project created for the "Game Matters" Game Jam.
