A Mannequin Tale

< Portfolio >

Roles: 🖥️Programmer,💡Game Designer,📊Level Designer

Overview:

A Mannequin Tale is a 3D platformer featuring a ragdoll-based movement system. The experience places a strong emphasis on player precision and reaction time, requiring accurate timing and spatial awareness to successfully navigate each level.

Play it!

Main contributions:

  • Co-designed the main game mechanics.
  • Designed, built, and tested some of the levels
  • Designed most of the obstacles handling their
  • creation and implementation in the game.
  • Customized the driving system to meet the project’s specific requirements.
  • Designed custom Blueprint methods to optimize game performance.
