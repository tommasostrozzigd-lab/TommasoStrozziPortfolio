Roles: 🖥️Programmer,💡Game Designer,
📜Narrative Designer

Quote:

“The actors have withdrawn, and Jane Doe is forced to take on every role, keeping the performance alive beat by beat.

As the show unfolds, music and identity begin to blur, each act demanding more of her.

By the time the final curtain falls, the play is complete but Jane is no longer herself.”



“... until you make it” is a narrative side-scrolling rhythm game.



This game was submitted for Global Game Jam 2026.
