Roles: 🖥️Programmer,💡Game Designer

Overview:

“Spinny” is a third-person 3D action-puzzle game with an isometric perspective.

The player controls Spinny, a robot that lands on Earth to collect parts needed to build a shuttle. The environment is hostile, with local entities acting as obstacles or enemies.

The game features a series of challenges that the player overcomes using movement abilities and upgradeable robotic skills acquired throughout progression.
