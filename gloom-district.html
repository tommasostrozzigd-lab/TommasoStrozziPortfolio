Gloom District

< Portfolio >

Roles: 🖥️Programmer,💡Game Designer

Overview:

A 2D point-and-click mystery game with branching dialogue and puzzle-solving mechanics.

The player uses the mouse to navigate the environment, interact with objects and solve logic-based puzzles and minigames to progress through the narrative.

The game features a character-switching system, allowing the player to alternate between the Detective and the Killer using the space bar. The two characters exist in separate timelines, and information discovered in one perspective is required to solve puzzles and advance in the other.
