Roles: 🖥️Programmer,💡Game Designer,
📊Level Designer,📜Narrative Designer

Overview:

The game presents itself as an action platformer inspired by the first book of the Aeneid, featuring predominantly absurd and comedic narrative elements.

Fuga Da Cag'Na

Main contributions:

  • Programmed a basic custom dialogue system for the game.
  • Designed and implemented the framework of main menu and pause menu UI.
  • Co-designed and implemented many of the game’s main mechanics.
  • Co-wrote the game’s story.
  • Designed and built the game’s levels.
  • Designed the scenes setup.
  • Designed and implemented enemy AI using a combination of behavior trees and custom scripting.
