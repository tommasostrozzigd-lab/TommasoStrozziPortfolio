ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Tommaso Strozzi. I am a graduate game designer and programmer at SAE Institute Milan. From the age of eight, I knew I wanted to create video games, and starting from 2013, my determination guided my studies in IT throughout middle and high school.

I have been actively developing games since 2021. Throughout my projects, I have consistently explored different programming techniques and game genres. This approach allows me to combine my two main interests: studying a target audience to craft engaging and meaningful gameplay experiences and finding practical solutions to bring the ideas to life.
